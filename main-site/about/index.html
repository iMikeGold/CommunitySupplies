About Us

Built by creators, for creators — the supply hub that understands the work you do and the work you put in.

Who We Are

Community Supplies is a specialist supply hub serving artists, makers, production teams, and creative practitioners of every kind. We are not a general store — we are built entirely around the work you do.

Whether you’re recording an album, building a set, running a workshop, or creating by hand, we stock the tools, materials, and equipment you rely on. Our range covers everything from pro audio and camera gear to raw materials, digital tools, and stage supplies — with options to buy new, buy pre‑owned, or rent for as long as you need.

Our Ethos

Rooted in community supplied,
community supported,
community shared for all. We believe good work depends on good access — and that access should be fair, reliable, and built together.

Our Purpose

We exist to keep creative work moving. We don’t talk about “empowering creativity” — we make sure the gear and materials are there when you turn up to work. Practical, honest, and focused entirely on what you actually need.

Our Approach

  • Stock relevant to real creative practice
  • New, second‑hand and rental options
  • Sustainable materials wherever possible
  • Part of the community we serve

Built for Practice, Not Just Purchase

We understand the process

Every category we stock comes from knowing exactly how creators work — from first idea to final output. We don’t list everything possible; we list what actually gets used, tested, and trusted in studios, workshops, and on stages.

More than a shop

We are infrastructure for creativity. When you trade with us, you’re supporting a system designed to keep skills, materials, and equipment circulating within the creative community.

Our Mission

To keep creative work moving — by providing accessible, high‑quality tools, materials and resources tailored exactly to how you work. We exist to serve makers, not just sell to them.
